Hangman Game Instructions

Welcome to the Hangman game! Here's how to play:

  • Enter a single letter in each box to guess the word.
  • If the letter is correct and in the right position, the box will turn green.
  • If the letter is in the word but in the wrong position, the box will turn yellow.
  • Keep guessing until you find the whole word.
  • New rows will be added for each guess.
